--- RULES ---

1. The number must be in the range between 1 and 48.
2. The numbers you place on your ticket must not repeat; they must be unique.
3. Once you have selected all the numbers, calculate the sum of the amount of 
money you want to bet on your ticket.
4. After placing your bet, keep track of the drawn numbers.
5. You can win only if you correctly guess all 6 numbers. Even if you don't match any, 
there is a prize for the lucky loser with odds of x1500 (the money you bet * 1500).
